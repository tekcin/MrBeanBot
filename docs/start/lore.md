# The Story of MrBeanBot

MrBeanBot started as a WhatsApp gateway project called **Warelay**. Over time it grew into a full multi-platform AI assistant.

## Timeline

- **2025**: Initial development as a WhatsApp + Telegram + Discord gateway for AI agents.
- **January 2026**: Rebranded to **MrBeanBot**, a personal AI assistant by Michael Thornton.

## What It Does

MrBeanBot bridges messaging platforms (WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, iMessage, Slack, Signal, and more) to AI coding agents like Pi. It runs on your own devices, answers on the channels you already use, and gives you a personal always-on assistant.
